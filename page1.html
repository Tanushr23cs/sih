About GLOFs

Glacial Lake Outburst Floods (GLOFs) occur when a glacial lake dam, formed by a glacier, fails suddenly, causing a massive release of water and debris downstream. These floods can be catastrophic, leading to loss of life, property damage, and disruption of ecosystems.

Always move to higher ground and avoid low-lying areas.

Stay away from floodwaters, which may be contaminated or contain debris.

Ensure you have an emergency kit with essential supplies.
